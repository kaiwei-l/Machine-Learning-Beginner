### Gradient ascent
* Definition
  * If we want to find the maximum point on a function, then the best way to move is in the direction of the gradient
* Formula
  * $\nabla f(x, y) = \begin{matrix} a \ b \end{matrix}$
$$
        \begin{matrix}
        1 & x & x^2 \\
        1 & y & y^2 \\
        1 & z & z^2 \\
        \end{matrix}
$$
